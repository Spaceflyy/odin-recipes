Korean Fried Chicken

Description

Korean fried chick is a popular dish amongst vistors and natives alike.

It comes in all shapes and sizes and is a great companion to some beer

so if you're looking for some fast and delicious food this is the dish for you!

Ingredients

For the chicken
  • 500g chicken wings
  • Large chunk of ginger, finely grated
  • 50g cornflour
  • Vegetable oil, for frying
  • Sesame seeds
  • Sliced spring onion
